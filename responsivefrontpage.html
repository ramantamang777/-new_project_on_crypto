Features

Incentivised Holding

"A portion of the transaction fee is meant to reward and incentivize those that choose to hold CURIC and secure the health of the project. RFI static rewards are used to give holders automatic reflections of BNB in their wallet simply by holding the token. Of the 13% transaction fee, 5% is distributed to holders automatically in the form of BNB. In the future, we hope our early investors will benefit from holding long-term by owning a token that can be used in our ecosystem as it expands in the future."

Blockchain

"$CURIC will harness the power of Binance Smart Chain (BSC) and blockchain technology to provide their users with distributed ledger technology. The BSC offers an extremely large user base because of their EVM compatibility and provides the same smart contract capabilities as Ethereum because they are an Ethereum- compatible blockchain. Despite being somewhat centralized, BSC provides users with lightning-fast transaction speeds and the ability to access tokens and products through multiple wallets. The extremely low fees in comparison to Ethereum make the BSC an affordable option for our investors."

Automatic Liquidity Pool (LP)

"Each transaction fee contributes 2% toward automatically generating further liquidity for investors. This benefits those that choose to hold $CURIC long-term. Of the 13% tax on each transaction, 2% is added to the liquidity pool through the contract. Liquidity is the measure that ensures investors can always buy and sell CURIC, and by increasing liquidity with each trade, MerCuric provides their investors with a healthy token and chart that will continue a growth trajectory well into the future burn mechanism. The smart contract will contain a burn mechanism to slowly deflate the token supply over time. With a 3% buy back and burn built contract during the sell"
